Getting into Table Top Role Playing Games

Modern Advent of the TTRPG

Table top role playing games, more often known as TTRPGs, have gained a massive surge of notoriety and attention in recent years, largly due to many celebrities, voice actors, and influencers producing shows and content revolving around them. There has been a flood of shows like Vox Machina or Harmon Quest, or YouTube channels like Critical Role and Dimension 20, and these have brought what was once a taboo hobby into the realm of pop cultur.
